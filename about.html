About Us

Founded in 2005, Aboutime is a leading provider of cleaning services to homes and businesses in the greater Dublin area. With an outstanding reputation for quality and integrity, we provide high quality cleaning and janitorial services tailored to our clients ‘ individual requirements. By choosing Aboutime you can be assured of exacting standards and utmost dependability at a reasonable cost.

We‘re in business for fourteen years and we‘ve seen it all – just try us! 70% of our business is either repeat or referral – our clients keep coming back and they tell their friends and colleagues too. We are so confident in our service that we don‘t need to tie you to a contract. Use us as long as you are happy with us!
