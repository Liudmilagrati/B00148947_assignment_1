Our Cleaning Process

Stress-free, Zen Cleaning!

Office and phone lines open Monday to Friday 8.30am – 5.00pm.

Once you have made initial contact with us, we will discuss your requirements and tailor a service to suit your needs. We may make an appointment to view your home or premises. One or more of our cleaners will call to your home on the agreed day with all necessary equipment and cleaning supplies or will use your own, if you prefer.

Many clients entrust us with both keys and alarm codes while others prefer to be present during a visit. Both arrangements are perfectly acceptable for us and our staff.

Aboutime provides a professional cleaning service to our clients and to this end our staff are uniformed and fully insured with both public liability and employer liability coverage. Staff are vetted at the time of employment and monitored to ensure the utmost security for our clients.

Next step will be when you get to put your energy into doing the things that are important to you while we look after the cleaning!
